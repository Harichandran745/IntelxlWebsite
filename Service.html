Our Services

Knowledge combined with quality gives our clients the edge over other competitors. Value assurance practices and optimized delivery cycles has been our focus from day one.

Technology that work for you

At IntelXL, we work with an understanding that technology is no longer a choice for businesses, but unarguable the most essential element behind the success of any enterprise. We take pride in our offshore development centre model that allows us to let businesses benefit from the most efficient technology and solutions, created and supported by a dedicated team of the most competent and qualified professionals – all that at a price that is highly competitive and reasonable.

Offering technology as a service also allows us to tailor everything right according to your needs as well as your budget. Our services cover a wide array of services mainly including the following.

Enterprise Application Development

Enterprise Application Development focuses on building secure, scalable software tailored for business needs, handling complex processes and large data. It ensures reliability, integration, and performance for critical organizational functions.

Medical Coding Service

Medical Coding Service involves translating healthcare diagnoses, procedures, and services into standardized codes. These codes enable accurate billing, insurance processing, and record-keeping for healthcare providers.

Enterprise Content Management Systems

Enterprise Content Management Systems (ECMS) enable organizations to manage, store, and organize digital content efficiently. They streamline workflows, enhance collaboration, and ensure secure access to information across the organization.

Enterprise Mobile Application Development

Enterprise Mobile Application Development involves creating secure, scalable mobile apps that support business operations, enabling employees and customers to access services on the go. These apps prioritize seamless integration, data security, and optimized performance for enterprise workflows.

Automated Software Testing

Automated Software Testing uses specialized tools to execute test cases and validate software functionality, reducing manual effort and increasing accuracy. It enables faster testing cycles, improves test coverage, and ensures consistent results for reliable software quality.

Cloud Computing Services

Cloud Computing Services provide on-demand access to computing resources like storage, processing power, and applications over the internet. They enable scalable, cost-effective solutions for data storage, software deployment, and IT infrastructure management.

Media Supporting Service

Media Supporting Service provides tools and infrastructure to manage, store, and deliver multimedia content, ensuring high-quality and efficient media distribution. It supports content streaming, digital asset management, and format compatibility for diverse platforms.
